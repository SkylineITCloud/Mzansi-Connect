# ICASA Compliance — Radio Frequency Spectrum (South Africa)

*Independent Communications Authority of South Africa Act 13 of 2000*

---

## 1. Applicable Regulations

Mzansi Connect IoT products use radio modules under ICASA jurisdiction:

| Module | Frequency Band | Classification |
| --- | --- | --- |
| ESP32 (WiFi) | 2.4 GHz ISM | Licence-exempt (Reg 6) |
| SIM800L (GSM) | 900/1800 MHz | Network-dependent |
| NEO-6M (GPS) | 1.57542 GHz L1 | Receive-only |

## 2. Compliance Obligations

- All transmitting modules must bear ICASA type-approval marking
- Licence-exempt operation: EIRP ≤ 10 mW/MHz, no harmful interference
- GSM operation requires valid SIM from licensed SA network operator

## 3. Labelling

> **ICASA COMPLIANCE**
> This device contains ICASA type-approved radio modules.
> WiFi: 2.4 GHz ISM band, EIRP ≤ 100 mW.
> GSM: 900/1800 MHz, network-dependent.
> Complies with ICASA Radio Frequency Spectrum Regulations, 2015.
