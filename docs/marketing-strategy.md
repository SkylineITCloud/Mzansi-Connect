## 4. Marketing Funnel

### 4.1 Awareness Stage

**Goal:** 10,000 people aware of Mzansi Connect in Year 1

**Tactics:**
- Social media ads targeting township locations
- Community radio interviews
- Partner endorsement from Skyline IT
- Referral program ("Tell a neighbour, earn R50")

**KPIs:**
- Social media impressions: 500,000+
- Website visitors: 20,000
- WhatsApp contacts added: 5,000

### 4.2 Consideration Stage

**Goal:** 2,000 people actively considering purchase

**Tactics:**
- Free demo events at township markets
- Video testimonials from pilot users
- Try before you buy (7-day free trial for spaza shops)
- Comparison with imported alternatives (SA vs. import)

**KPIs:**
- Demo attendees: 2,000
- Free trial signups: 500
- Website product page views: 8,000

### 4.3 Conversion Stage

**Goal:** 1,500 sales in Year 1

**Tactics:**
- Launch pricing (10% off first 500 orders)
- Bundle discounts (security + water = save R150)
- Payment plans (R99/month for 10 months)
- Money-back guarantee (30 days)

**KPIs:**
- Conversion rate: 7.5% (of considerers)
- Cart completion rate: >60%
- Average order value: R899

### 4.4 Retention Stage

**Goal:** 60% repeat purchase / subscription rate

**Tactics:**
- Subscription discounts for returning customers
- Referral rewards (R50 per successful referral)
- Regular WhatsApp tips and product updates
- VIP community group for product owners

**KPIs:**
- Repeat purchase rate: 25%
- Subscription renewal: 70%
- Net Promoter Score: >40
