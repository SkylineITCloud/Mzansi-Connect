## 2. Target Market Deep-Dive

### 2.1 Spaza Shop Owners

**Addressable Market:** ~250,000 spaza shops nationally

**Demographics:**
- Owner age: 25–45 (average)
- Monthly revenue: R15,000–R80,000
- Tech familiarity: Moderate (majority use smartphones)
- Key concerns: Theft (62%), stock management (28%), electricity costs (10%)

**Willingness to pay:** R500–R1,500 for security solution. 73% of surveyed owners said they would pay up to R1,000 for a system that reduces theft.

**Purchase drivers:**
- Word of mouth (most trusted)
- WhatsApp demos
- Community endorsements
- Free trial / money-back guarantee

### 2.2 Township Households

**Addressable Market:** ~7 million households

**Segments:**
| Segment | Size | Key Need | Price Sensitivity |
|---------|------|----------|-----------------|
| Prepaid electricity users | 5M | Usage tracking, budgeting | High |
| JoJo tank owners | 3M | Water level monitoring | Medium |
| Backyard tenants | 2M | Usage-based billing | Medium |
| Homeowners (middle-income) | 1.5M | Smart security | Low |

### 2.3 Minibus Taxi Operators

**Addressable Market:** ~150,000 vehicles

**Pain Points:**
- Fuel theft (estimated 15–25% fuel loss)
- Route inefficiency
- Driver behavior (speeding, harsh braking)
- Vehicle maintenance tracking

**Willingness to pay:** R1,000–R2,000 upfront + R150–R250/month subscription. 45% of operators surveyed expressed interest in affordable tracking.

### 2.4 Schools (Smart School Kit)

**Addressable Market:** 25,000+ schools in SA

**Key Entry Point:** No-fee schools in townships (government-funded, but need affordable tech solutions)

## 5. Customer Insights

### 5.1 Survey Data Summary

Based on informal surveys conducted in Durban townships (n=87):

- **78%** of spaza shop owners experienced theft in the past year
- **64%** use prepaid electricity and want better tracking
- **59%** have a JoJo tank but no level monitoring
- **81%** prefer WhatsApp-based support over phone/email
- **72%** would recommend a product to a neighbour if satisfied
- **Average acceptable price** for a security device: R850
