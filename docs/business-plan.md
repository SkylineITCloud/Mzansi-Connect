# Business Plan — Mzansi Connect

**A subsidiary of Skyline IT & Cloud**  
**Date:** June 2026

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Mzansi Connect is a South African IoT technology company that develops affordable smart devices for townships, rural communities, small businesses, and schools. By leveraging low-cost microcontrollers (ESP32), GSM/LoRa connectivity, and solar power, we deliver practical solutions for security, water monitoring, energy management, and fleet tracking at 40–60% less than imported alternatives.

**Mission:** Smart Technology for Every South African.  
**Vision:** To become Africa's leading affordable IoT provider by 2035.  
**Motto:** Connecting Every South African to the Future.

## 6. Operations

### 6.1 Manufacturing Process

1. **PCB Assembly** — Outsourced to PCBWay (5–10 day turnaround)
2. **Firmware Flashing** — In-house using ESP32 programmer jig
3. **Assembly** — Enclosure + PCB + sensors assembled locally
4. **Testing** — Each unit tested for 24-hour burn-in
5. **Packaging** — Branded box with quick-start guide
6. **Dispatch** — Via Courier Guy / Pudo for last-mile delivery

### 6.2 Quality Assurance

- 100% functional testing before shipping
- 24-hour burn-in test for first 500 units
- 1-year warranty on all hardware
- OTA firmware updates via ESP32 OTA

### 6.3 Customer Support
